## The AI Readiness Framework

We evaluate businesses across five dimensions:

1. Data Centralization
2. Data Quality
3. Process Documentation
4. Technical Infrastructure
5. Team Readiness

Let's assess each one.

## 1. Data Centralization Assessment

**Ask yourself:**

- Can you get a complete view of any customer in under 60 seconds?
- Is your customer data in one place, or scattered across multiple tools?
- Do you have a single source of truth for projects/jobs?
- Can you pull reports without manually exporting from multiple systems?

## 2. Data Quality Assessment

**Ask yourself:**

- How often do you find duplicate records?
- Is data entry consistent (same format for phone numbers, addresses, etc.)?
- Are old/inactive records properly archived?
- Do you have data validation rules in place?

## 3. Process Documentation Assessment

**Ask yourself:**

- Are your core business processes documented?
- Could a new hire follow the documentation to complete key tasks?

**Ask yourself:**

## The Path to AI Readiness

### If you scored 5-8 points:

**Priority actions:**
1. Conduct a full software stack audit
2. Identify your 3-5 core systems of record
3. Create a data centralization plan
4. Implement basic data quality controls
5. Document your core processes

**Timeline**: 3-6 months of focused effort

### If you scored 9-12 points:

**Priority actions:**
1. Close remaining integration gaps
2. Deep-clean your data
3. Complete process documentation
4. Build internal AI awareness
5. Identify your first AI use case

**Timeline**: 1-3 months to AI-ready

### If you scored 13-15 points:

**Priority actions:**
1. Identify high-impact AI use cases
2. Run pilot projects
3. Measure results and iterate
4. Scale successful implementations
5. Build AI into standard operations

**Timeline**: Start now!
